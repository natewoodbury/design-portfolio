BRICK

Brick is a real-estate startup focused on creating new opportunities for underserved real-estate markets. I led the development and design of three integrated products:

My Brick Offer - A consumer-facing platform for presenting and transacting real estate offers in clear concise terms. Featured an API integration with DocuSign and a messaging system to facilitate addendums.

Brick Kiln - An internal CRM platform for evaluating and underwriting real-estate properties. This featured integrations with DocuSign, 3rd Party analytics, and email API platforms. Also included features for inventory management, internal messaging, and project management tools.

Brick Marketplace - An informational product for displaying and browsing real estate currently held by Brick. Utilized e-commerce-like features as well as email marketing integrations.

Biofire Defense

Biofire Defense builds pathogen detection systems for local, state, and federal US agencies. These machines and the software that runs them are subject to stringent regulations that need to be navigated while attempting to build user-centric software. Due to the nature of these products, I am limited in the designs I am able to share publicly.

I was the lead designer for the core software update of The Razor Mk II detection system. This comprehensive effort involved initial sketches, in-depth research, and analysis sourced from our customer support department, culminating in the successful release of an updated system. The result was a notable reduction in user errors, coupled with enhanced safety and precision in system operation. A critical success when dealing with contagious pathogens.

Created user-centric web interfaces to visually present complex diagnostic data and analytics, enhancing nationwide awareness of pathogen trends and promoting data transparency.
